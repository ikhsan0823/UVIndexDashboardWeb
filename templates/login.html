Use AutoLocate ?

The automatic search feature utilizes location tracking technology to provide UV index information based on your current location. The system will automatically retrieve your location and provide instant information about the UV index in that area. This feature is particularly convenient for users who want quick information without the need to manually enter an address.

Input address ?

The manual search feature allows you to enter an address directly into the search form. This gives you full control over the location you want to check for UV index. Ideally, you can find out the UV exposure level in a specific area. Simply input the address you want to examine, and our system will provide you with up-to-date information on the UV index in that region.
